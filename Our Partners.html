Company History:

- Oxey is founded in the USA, by Svaloknok K. in 1957. In the same year we open our first factory making first pharmaceutical equidment.
- In 1962 after 5 despite having high profits the company still faces heavy financial troubles for being forced to buy resources for a high prices. For this the company shifts its interest and makes the decision to start making military issue equidment for the NATO.
-Our company participates in several wars in the upcoming years however for security reasons we have to conceal this information on which wars were these.
-The company acquires several factories in these countries: Brazilian State,State of Bolivia,Democratic Republic of the Congo, French Algeria,Republic of South Africa,United States of Oceania (This mainly means the old country called "New Zealand")
-The company reaches its first golden age as it gets several contracts by the NATO countries.
-However after the crisis of the 70's our company once again faces financial issues and must expand to survive. For this our company starts a combined development with several other companies to develop a new technology to face the Eastern Continent.
-This came to fruition in 1996, as the creation of biomechanical augmentation creates a new age for humanity. This year marks the start of First Generation Augmentations.
-With this new found golden age our company begun expanding, building factories across all NATO member countries.
-2005 marks the year of the begining of Star Wars where the NATO and ECA races towards space dominance.
-With 2006 marks the Second Generation of Augmentation with smaller more useful, compact products.
-2016 marks the Third generation of Augmentation from here on our company begins selling first generation and second generation products to the civilian populace
-Currently our company in 2025 has more then $200 Billion dollars worth of assets with a soaring stock.
